About this service

Contract staffing places qualified professionals with your organization for a defined period, contributing the skills your projects require without the overhead of a permanent hire.

At Masota Corporation, we match organizations with screened, qualified contract professionals across IT, Engineering, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Logistics, and more. Our quality-first approach means every candidate is evaluated before you meet them.

Key Points
  • Scale quickly for a project or deadline
  • Cover extended leave or temporary vacancies
  • Access specialized skills on a flexible timeline
  • Manage seasonal or fluctuating workforce demand
  • Evaluate candidates before making a permanent offer
  • Stay agile without long-term overhead
Engagement Types

Flexible options for your situation

Short-Term

Weeks to months. Cover gaps, surges, and deadlines without long-term obligations.

Project-Based

Defined scope, defined team. Bring in specialists for the duration of a project.

Contract-to-Hire

Evaluate professionals on the job before making a permanent offer — reduce hiring risk.
