Products

4 questions

Masota's flagship products are TabEmail — a premium email campaign management platform — and TabRecruit — a recruitment CRM and talent acquisition platform. Both are available at tabemail.com and tabrecruit.com respectively.

TabEmail is Masota's premium email communication and campaign management platform. It includes a unified inbox, multi-account support, campaign automation, follow-up sequences, sender rotation, analytics, tracking and advanced segmentation. It is built for organizations that demand high deliverability and intelligent automation at scale.

TabRecruit is Masota's recruitment CRM and talent acquisition platform. It supports candidate pipelines, job management, workflow automation, team collaboration, freelance marketplace access and vendor management — built for organizations that hire at scale and need full pipeline transparency.

TabEmail and TabRecruit are purpose-built products operated by Masota Corporation — not white-labeled third-party tools. They are designed around real-world recruitment and marketing workflows, and are continuously developed by Masota's own engineering team based on client feedback and market needs.

TabEmail

5 questions

TabEmail is a comprehensive email campaign management platform that enables organizations to run sophisticated outreach and marketing programs. It supports unified inbox management, multi-account email connectivity, campaign creation, automation sequences, sender rotation to maximize deliverability, follow-up workflows and full campaign analytics.

Yes. TabEmail supports multi-account connectivity, allowing organizations and teams to manage multiple sender accounts from a single platform. This is particularly valuable for outbound sales teams, marketing agencies and organizations running campaigns across multiple brands or business units.

Yes. TabEmail includes a full campaign automation suite — enabling organizations to build multi-step drip sequences, trigger-based follow-ups, time-delayed sends and behavioral automation rules. Campaigns can be set up once and run automatically based on recipient behavior and defined schedules.

Yes. TabEmail provides comprehensive tracking including open tracking, click tracking, link-level reporting, reply tracking and campaign-level analytics. All data is available in real-time dashboards so organizations can monitor performance and optimize campaigns continuously.

Yes. Follow-up sequences are a core feature of TabEmail. Organizations can configure automated follow-up chains triggered by recipient behavior — including non-opens, non-clicks or specific link engagements — ensuring no prospect falls through the cracks without requiring manual intervention.

TabRecruit

4 questions

TabRecruit is Masota's purpose-built recruitment CRM and talent acquisition platform. It enables recruitment teams and HR organizations to manage the complete hiring lifecycle — from job posting and candidate sourcing through pipeline management, interview scheduling, offer management and analytics.

Yes. TabRecruit provides full candidate pipeline management with customizable stages, drag-and-drop workflow boards, candidate status tracking and automated stage-progression notifications. Teams can see every candidate's status across every active role in real time.

Yes. TabRecruit supports workflow automation including automated candidate status updates, email notification triggers, interview scheduling workflows, follow-up reminders and pipeline stage automation. This reduces manual administrative work and keeps hiring processes moving efficiently.

Yes. TabRecruit is built for multi-user recruitment teams with role-based access controls, shared candidate pools, team activity tracking, collaborative notes and candidate ownership assignment. It is equally suited for in-house HR teams, staffing agencies and RPO organizations.
