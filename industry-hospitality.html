Industry Challenges

What makes Hospitality unique

Chronic Workforce Shortage

Hospitality has faced persistent staffing shortages since the pandemic — making proactive, continuous sourcing essential for operations stability.

High Turnover

Front-line hospitality turnover can exceed 70% annually — requiring high-volume, fast-fill recruiting that maintains consistent service quality.

Guest Experience Expectations

Guests now expect personalized, digital-first experiences — requiring investment in both technology and trained staff.

Seasonal Demand Volatility

Occupancy and covers fluctuate dramatically by season — requiring flexible staffing models that scale up and down without disruption.

Direct Booking Competition

OTA dominance continues to erode direct booking rates — requiring sophisticated email marketing and SEO to recapture direct revenue.

F&B Complexity

Managing multiple F&B outlets requires specialized culinary, operations and management talent that is increasingly difficult to source.
